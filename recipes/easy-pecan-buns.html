Easy Pecan Buns

Description

Made with just Hawaiian rolls, pecans, and a homemade sticky sauce, this 30-minute breakfast couldn't be easier to make at home. Talk about sweet!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
  • 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (12-count)-package Hawaiian rolls

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a 8x8 cake pan with parchment paper.
  2. Combine butter, sugar, salt, and honey in a pan set over medium heat. Stir while cooking until the sugar dissolves and mixture starts to bubble a little bit, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat and add vanilla bean paste, heavy cream, and chopped pecans. Mix until the pecans are fully coated and set aside to cool slightly.
  4. Pour all of the sauce in the pan into the prepared cake pan and spread it out into an even layer. Add Hawaiian rolls, top side down.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the sauce is very bubbly and the buns are slightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Let it cool for up to 5 minutes before flipping onto a cutting board or plate.
