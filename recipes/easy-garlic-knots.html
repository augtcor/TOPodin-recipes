Easy Garlic Knots

Description

These easy garlic knots are so good, you'll want to make an extra batch to take pizza night to the next level

  • 10 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup minced garlic (about 8 large cloves)
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a large baking sheet.
  2. Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface to a 10x16-inch rectangle. Cut dough lengthwise into 12 strips. Halve strips to make 24 (8-inch-long) strips. Tie each strip in a knot. Arrange on prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Transfer knots to a large bowl. Drizzle with oil. Sprinkle with garlic, Parmesan, parsley, crushed red pepper, and salt. Toss gently to coat.
